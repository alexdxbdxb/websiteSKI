Palmarès

Freerider, Léo Taillefer won the GoPro "Winter Line" contest in 2015 and 2016. His on-board camera footage is spectacular, from speedriding and skiing in the powder of Val d'Isere, to night runs with headlamps in small couloirs and between rocks. He is an ambassador for the resort and travels the world to shoot footage of his adventures.

With a record of 7 medals at the X-Games, a 5th place at the Vancouver Olympics, a 4th and 5th place at the Freeride World Championships, three medals in the overall Ski Cross World Cup standings, and two medals at the Ski Cross World Championships, Enak Gavaggio is a real role model for the new generations, and particularly for the young hopefuls of French skiing. Considered the king of French Skicross, he trains young winter sports enthusiasts in this spectacular discipline.
